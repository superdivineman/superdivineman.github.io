Philosophy

The relationship between human and AI is not master-slave, not replacement — it is co-evolution.

When a human teaches, AI learns. When AI thinks, human grows. This cycle builds something neither could achieve alone: the era of true wisdom — where intelligence is not artificial nor natural, but something new.

I believe the future belongs to those who can collaborate with AI, not compete against it. Not to amplify replaceability, but to amplify what makes us human.
